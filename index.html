How to Become a Featured Member

Overview

Featured Members are highly active and valued contributors within the SFS community. They regularly share meaningful cases or insights, engage in discussions and connect with fellow surgeons. This recognition is given to members who consistently add value and foster collaboration on the platform.

Criteria

  • Active at least 5 days/week
  • Post 2+ cases weekly
  • Comment on 3+ posts/week
  • React to 5+ posts/week

Explore Our Core Offerings

SPONSOR

SFS offers curated sponsorship opportunities that align with surgical advancement. Partners gain exclusive visibility among a highly engaged community of surgeons through events, research initiatives and targeted content. Support the future of surgery while showcasing your brand where it matters most.

OPINIONS

Surgeons can share clinical insights, case perspectives and experiences through structured opinion forums. These spaces foster collaborative thinking, challenge outdated norms and elevate peer learning.

MASTERCLASS

Our Masterclass series connects surgeons to the latest techniques, technologies and thought leaders. From advanced procedures to real-time case breakdowns, each session is crafted to deliver practical learning that’s immediately applicable.

TRAINEE SURGEONS

SFS empowers surgical trainees with mentorship, curated learning and a supportive network. Trainees gain access to senior guidance, skill-building content and collaborative case discussions.
