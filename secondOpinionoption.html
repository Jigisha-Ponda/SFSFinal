How to Join SFS and Submit Your Case

Download the SFS App – Get the official Surgeons for Surgeons app from Google Play or Apple App Store

Register Your Account - Sign up, verify your identity and become a confirmed SFS member

Complete Your Profile - Add speciality, qualifications and alma mater to connect with relevant groups

Join Relevant Groups - Join relevant specialty or interest groups for focused discussions

Post Your Case for Review - Share cases securely for peer feedback or a second opinion

Interact & Collaborate - Comment on cases, share insights and network with surgeons worldwide

Stay Updated - Enable alerts for cases, discussions and resources
